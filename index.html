Use Cautiosly!

You'll notice that other than the domain, your name is not listed anywhere on this website. That is because the source is made publicly available, and I want to protect your identity because I love you <3.

Don't mix online identities

You probably already have several online identities. Maybe you use the same username for fan sites, and a different one for social media. The internet gives the gift of anonimity,and it's important to understand when to use various levels of anonimity.

Because this domain is your name, anything that is attached to it is attached to your most public identity. Only use it for things that you actually want people to know who you are. Don't sign up for some random fan website with it. But do create an email address with it, and use it on a resume.
